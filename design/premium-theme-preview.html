Notes for review. Typography pairs a serif display face (Georgia-class; can be upgraded to a licensed premium serif) for page titles and recommendations with Inter for UI text. Gold is reserved for primary actions, active navigation, and the AI identity — never as ambient decoration. The particle-AI identity, motion rules, accessibility (WCAG 2.2 AA), and all component standards from the Experience & Design Bible remain unchanged — this proposal replaces the color system and zone layering only (Bible §9–10) and adds a serif display tier to §11. On your approval, document 04 will be updated to v3.1 with these tokens, and Sprint 1's Tailwind config will implement them.
